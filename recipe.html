Scrap Stir Fry

It's the end of the week. You are way too tired and way too hungry to go grocery shopping. Your fridge is a sad scape, wilty spinach and broccoli teasing you.
Take those veggies you would otherwise throw away and throw them into this scrap stir fry!
This vegetable meddley calls for absolutely whatever produce you already have, so no need to leave the house. It pairs perfect with a bowl of rice or some meat if you need additional protein.

Main page
Ingredients

Olive oil

Garlic cloves

Soy sauce

Peppers (Green, orange, red or yellow)

Carrots

Broccoli

Spinach

Peas

Cauliflower

Mushrooms

Any vegetable you have!

Instructions

1. Chop your vegetables and garlic cloves to your preference.
2. Add 2-3 tablespoons of olive oil to a large skillet over medium heat.
3. Add garlic cloves to hot skillet and let cook for a minute.
4. Add vegetables to skillet in order of what takes longest to cook. Items like carrots, onions and peppers typically take longer than mushrooms and spinach.
5. Let vegetables cook for 5-10 minutes.
6. Add soy sauce to taste and let cook for an additional 5 minutes.
*For softer vegetables, add a couple tablespoons of water and cover with a lid for several minutes to steam the vegetables.

This recipe is just a starting point. Let your imagination wander when thinking of how to put your food to use.
